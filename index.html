A bit about me...

I currenty reside in the beautiful state of Texas, in its weirdest city; Austin. I moved here from San Marcos, where I was pursuing and completed a Bachelor of Science in Psychology, with dual minors in Biology and Biochemistry at Texas State University. I found my love of development in these science classes, but the data was so dry. What I really wanted was to build something for myself, which I would actually use and enjoy. Joke-Teller-Bot

Web app demonstrating API usage. When the 'Tell me a joke' button is pressed, the JokeAPI is called, which returns a programming joke. This data is then issued to the VoiceRSS API, which will take the supplied data and read out the joke. All jokes are prgramming related, and Safe For Work! (Be sure to check your device's volume settings though to avoid startles.)
